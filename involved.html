    • Get Involved


    Join us in making a difference! At Lawrence Memorial Humanitarian Foundation, we believe that together, we can create a brighter future for the less privileged and struggling communities. Whether you’re an individual, organization, or business, there are many ways to support our mission. Every contribution, no matter how small, brings hope and transforms lives. Together, we can uplift communities and build a better tomorrow. Be the change—get involved today!




    Pad The Girl Child


    Nov 20th,2024 | Enugu, Nigeria

    Empowering Girls, Protecting Futures. The Pad the Girl Child Program is our initiative dedicated to supporting young girls by providing access to essential menstrual hygiene products and education. We believe that no girl should miss out on education or opportunities due to a lack of resources. Together, we can ensure every girl has the confidence and dignity to pursue her dreams. Join us in creating a brighter, more inclusive future for the girl child!

    Medical Health Outreach


    Aug 25th,2022 | Enugu, Nigeria

    Our Medical Health Outreach program is dedicated to improving the health and well-being of underserved communities by providing access to essential medical care and health education. Recognizing that health is the foundation of a thriving society, we bring vital healthcare services directly to those who need them most. With the support of our dedicated medical professionals and volunteers, the Medical Health Outreach program is making a meaningful impact, ensuring that no one is left behind in the journey toward better health. Together, we can build healthier, stronger communities.

    Accomplished Programs



    Celebrating Impactful Achievements

    Our accomplished programs showcase the transformative initiatives that have brought hope, change, and empowerment to the communities we serve. These milestones reflect our unwavering commitment to making a meaningful difference.


    Quiz Competition (Online)

    Our Quiz Competition Online program is designed to engage young minds, foster learning, and promote academic excellence. This interactive initiative provides students with an opportunity to showcase their knowledge across various subjects while encouraging healthy competition. By leveraging technology, we create a platform for intellectual growth and skill-building that is accessible to all.

    Health Awareness Competition (Online)

    The Health Awareness Online program aims to educate individuals on key health issues and promote preventive care through virtual sessions, workshops, and resources. From mental health to chronic disease prevention, this program uses digital platforms to reach a broader audience, empowering communities to make informed decisions about their well-being.


    View More

    Food Pantry Program

    Our Food Pantry Program provides essential food supplies to individuals and families experiencing food insecurity. By partnering with donors and community stakeholders, we ensure that no one goes hungry. This program is a lifeline for many, offering nourishment and hope to those in need.


    View More

    Family Support Outreach Program

    The Family Support Outreach program is dedicated to uplifting struggling families by providing financial assistance, counseling, and essential resources. We aim to strengthen families and create a foundation of stability, helping them overcome challenges and achieve lasting progress.


    View More

    Student Support & Scholarship Program

    Through the Student Support and Scholarship Program, we invest in the future by supporting students with educational resources, mentorship, and scholarships. This program is designed to remove barriers to education, empowering students to excel academically and realize their potential.


    View More

    Pad The Girl Child

    The Pad the Girl Child initiative addresses menstrual hygiene challenges faced by young girls. By providing free sanitary pads and health education, this program ensures that no girl is held back by her period. It aims to promote dignity, confidence, and equal opportunities for girls in underserved communities.


    View More

    Free Medical Outreach

    Our Free Medical Outreach program brings essential healthcare services directly to communities in need. From free check-ups and treatments to distributing medications and health resources, this program ensures that everyone has access to quality medical care, regardless of their circumstances.


    View More

    Youth & Young Adults

    Our organization is dedicated to empowering youth and young adults by providing resources, guidance, and opportunities for personal growth and development. We offer a variety of programs that focus on education, leadership, skill-building, and mental health support, helping young people navigate the challenges they face as they transition into adulthood.


    View More

    Youth Empowerment

    Youth Empowerment Foundation (YEF) is a non-governmental organization dedicated to supporting the personal and professional development of youth and young adults. Through mentorship programs, leadership training, and community engagement initiatives, YEF helps young people develop essential skills, build confidence, and unlock their potential.


    View More

    Skill Acquisition

    Our organization is committed to empowering youth and young adults by providing access to skill acquisition programs that enhance their personal and professional development. We offer hands-on training in areas such as technology, entrepreneurship, creative arts, communication, and leadership, designed to equip young people with the practical skills needed to succeed in today’s rapidly changing world.


    View More

    Mental Health Outreach

    Our organization is dedicated to supporting youth and young adults through mental health awareness and outreach programs. We focus on providing education, resources, and counseling to help young people better understand and manage their mental well-being


    View More

    Community Development

    Our organization is dedicated to fostering community development by empowering youth and young adults to become active participants in shaping their communities. Through a range of programs focused on civic engagement, volunteerism, and leadership development, we provide young people with the skills and opportunities to address local challenges and contribute to positive social change.


    View More

    Counseling & Rehaibilitation

    Our organization offers counseling and rehabilitation services to support youth and young adults facing mental health challenges, substance abuse, and emotional difficulties. Through professional counseling, therapy sessions, and personalized rehabilitation programs, we provide a safe space for individuals to heal and recover.


    View More

    Borehole Water Project

    Our Borehole Water Project is dedicated to providing sustainable access to clean and safe drinking water for communities in need. Through the installation of boreholes in water-scarce areas, we aim to reduce water shortages and improve the overall health and well-being of local populations.
