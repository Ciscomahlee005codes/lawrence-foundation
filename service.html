    What We Do




    At Lawrence Memorial Humanitarian Foundation, we are driven by a mission to improve lives and create opportunities for underserved communities. We tackle pressing social issues through innovative and sustainable initiatives in healthcare, education, empowerment, and economic development. From providing immediate care to fostering long-term growth, we work to ensure that every individual we serve has the chance to live a healthier, more fulfilling life.


    We Find & Fund


    We identify and support projects that address critical needs in healthcare, education, and economic development. Our team of experts works closely with local partners to ensure that every dollar counts and every project has a lasting impact.

    We Provide Care


    Our healthcare initiatives focus on bringing essential medical services to underserved populations. From organizing medical outreaches to providing life-saving treatments, we work tirelessly to ensure that no one is left behind in accessing quality care.

    We Educate


    Education is the cornerstone of a thriving community. We support programs that enhance learning opportunities for children and adults, equipping them with knowledge and skills to build brighter futures and break the cycle of poverty.

    We Employ


    Empowerment begins with opportunity. We create pathways to sustainable livelihoods by supporting vocational training, job placement programs, and entrepreneurial ventures, helping individuals achieve financial independence and dignity.

    Medical Health Outreach


    Aug 25th,2022 | Enugu, Nigeria

    Our Medical Health Outreach program is dedicated to improving the health and well-being of underserved communities by providing access to essential medical care and health education. Recognizing that health is the foundation of a thriving society, we bring vital healthcare services directly to those who need them most. With the support of our dedicated medical professionals and volunteers, the Medical Health Outreach program is making a meaningful impact, ensuring that no one is left behind in the journey toward better health. Together, we can build healthier, stronger communities.

    Accomplished Programs



    Celebrating Impactful Achievements

    Our accomplished programs showcase the transformative initiatives that have brought hope, change, and empowerment to the communities we serve. These milestones reflect our unwavering commitment to making a meaningful difference.


    Quiz Competition (Online)

    Our Quiz Competition Online program is designed to engage young minds, foster learning, and promote academic excellence. This interactive initiative provides students with an opportunity to showcase their knowledge across various subjects while encouraging healthy competition. By leveraging technology, we create a platform for intellectual growth and skill-building that is accessible to all.

    Health Awareness Competition (Online)

    The Health Awareness Online program aims to educate individuals on key health issues and promote preventive care through virtual sessions, workshops, and resources. From mental health to chronic disease prevention, this program uses digital platforms to reach a broader audience, empowering communities to make informed decisions about their well-being.


    View More

    Food Pantry Program

    Our Food Pantry Program provides essential food supplies to individuals and families experiencing food insecurity. By partnering with donors and community stakeholders, we ensure that no one goes hungry. This program is a lifeline for many, offering nourishment and hope to those in need.


    View More

    Family Support Outreach Program

    The Family Support Outreach program is dedicated to uplifting struggling families by providing financial assistance, counseling, and essential resources. We aim to strengthen families and create a foundation of stability, helping them overcome challenges and achieve lasting progress.

    Student Support & Scholarship Program

    Through the Student Support and Scholarship Program, we invest in the future by supporting students with educational resources, mentorship, and scholarships. This program is designed to remove barriers to education, empowering students to excel academically and realize their potential.

    Pad a Girl Child

    The Pad the Girl Child initiative addresses menstrual hygiene challenges faced by young girls. By providing free sanitary pads and health education, this program ensures that no girl is held back by her period. It aims to promote dignity, confidence, and equal opportunities for girls in underserved communities.

    Free Medical Outreach

    Our Free Medical Outreach program brings essential healthcare services directly to communities in need. From free check-ups and treatments to distributing medications and health resources, this program ensures that everyone has access to quality medical care, regardless of their circumstances.
