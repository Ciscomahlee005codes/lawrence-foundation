    • Empowering Girls, One Pad at a Time


    Menstrual hygiene should never be a barrier to a girlâ€™s education or dignity. Through 'Pad the Girl Child,' we are committed to providing essential sanitary products and education about menstrual health to underprivileged girls, fostering confidence, health, and equality. Together, we can create a future where no girl is left behind. Through this initiative, we not only distribute sanitary pads but also organize workshops and mentorship programs to educate young girls on menstrual health and hygiene. By breaking the stigma surrounding menstruation, we aim to create safe spaces for girls to express their concerns and access support. This program is a step toward ensuring equal opportunities for education, growth, and empowerment, enabling girls to thrive without fear or shame
